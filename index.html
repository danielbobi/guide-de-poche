Emergency Contacts

Tap a number to dial. These work on any mobile device in France, even without local roaming enabled.

112 - European Emergency

Universal help line (English speakers available)
15 - SAMU (Medical Emergency)

Ambulance dispatch & critical health emergencies
17 - Police / Gendarmerie

To report crimes or secure safety help
18 - Fire & Accident Rescue

Sapeurs-Pompiers (medical rescue, road crashes)
